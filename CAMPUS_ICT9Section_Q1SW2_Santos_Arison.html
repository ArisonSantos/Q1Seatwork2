CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Maria Clara Full name Maria Clara de los Santos. The daughter of Capitan Tiago and the fiancee of Crisostomo Ibarra. Waiting for Ibarra's return to San Diego, Maria Clara faced numerous objections to their betrothal.
Padre Damaso The former town curate of San Diego and the real father of Maria Clara. Opposing his daughter's marriage to Crisostomo Ibarra, the man made efforts to drive the two apart for Maria Clara's sake.
Capitan Tiago Influential businessman in San Diego and the father of Maria Clara. Betrothing his daughter to Crisostomo Ibarra, Capitan Tiago struggled to obey the will of the friars.
